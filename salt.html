Salt

Season your speech with salt

CLIENT: Salt
DATE: September 2014
DRIBBBLE: VIEW

Salt is an ecommerce site designed to sell inspirational word art. You will find inspiring quotes from well-known humanitarians, the Bible, song lyrics, and much more. The customer can search, purchase, and find similar products.

I provided a color palette, logo design, and basic wireframes and designs. I chose a mustard yellow as the primary color because it is light and airy, and quite honestly reminds me of seasonings. Salt is a seasoning, so it works. The light blue reminded me of the sea water which is full of salt.The 2 colors worked together to make a beautiful combination that I was thrilled with. Finally, the search link is an expanding search bar, and in the final design portion, I made available the entire design.
